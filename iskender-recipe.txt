Ingredients:
 - 1/2 kg lean beef
 - 2 tablespoons yogurt
 - 1 pide bread
 - 50 grams of butter
 - spices

Directions:
  1. Add spices, yogurt, olive oil and sliced beef and cover for 15 mins.
  2. Place the marinated meat on high heat pan.
  3. Place cubed bread, then meat and finally melted butter on plate.
